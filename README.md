Classic Models Sales Analysis – Power BI
 Project Overview:-

This Power BI dashboard analyzes sales performance, product distribution, customer segmentation, and order trends using the Classic Models dataset.

The objective of this project is to identify revenue drivers, top-performing product categories, seasonal trends, and key influencing factors impacting sales.

Key KPIs

a)Total Sales: 561M+

b)Total Quantity Ordered: 146K+

c)Profit Margin: 6M+

#Top Selling Product: 1992 Ferrari 360 Spider Red

* Dashboard Insights
1️) Sales by Product Line:

Classic Cars contribute the highest revenue share.

Vintage Cars and Motorcycles follow as secondary contributors.

2️) Monthly & Yearly Trends:

Sales peaked in 2004.

Decline observed in 2005 indicating potential demand slowdown.

3️) Customer Distribution (Geographical Analysis)

Majority customers located in North America and Europe.

Opportunity for market expansion in Asia.

4️) AI Visual – Key Influencers

Product type significantly impacts sales growth.

Ferrari Enzo and Alpine Renault models show strong influence on revenue increase.
